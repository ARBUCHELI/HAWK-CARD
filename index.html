Hawk

Hawk is often a messenger from Angels, Devas and the Divine. He signals a time in your life when you need to focus on what's ahead and prepare for a leadership role

  • Scientific Name: Buteo jamaicensis
  • Body Lenght: 45 - 65 cm
  • Lifespan: 21 years
  • Habitat: Any

Hawks are a group of medium-sized diurnal birds of prey of the family Accipitridae. Hawks are widely distributed and vary greatly in size.
